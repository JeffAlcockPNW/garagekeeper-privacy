GarageKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

GarageKeeper is a personal vehicle-maintenance tracker. It is designed to keep your data private by keeping it on your device. This policy explains what information the app handles and what leaves your device.

Summary

  • GarageKeeper does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.
  • All of your data is stored locally on your device.
  • There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.
  • The only information that ever leaves your device is a vehicle make and model-year you enter, sent to a public U.S. government lookup service to fill in matching model names.

Information you enter

GarageKeeper lets you record vehicles and their maintenance history — for example a vehicle's make, model, year, color, and mileage, and service records such as the date, mileage, service type, shop, cost, and notes. This information is stored only on your device and is never sent to the developer or to any server.

Vehicle model lookup (the only outbound request)

When you add a vehicle, GarageKeeper can look up matching model names to make data entry easier. To do this, the make and model-year you type are sent to the U.S. National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) public vPIC API, which returns a list of models. This request contains only the vehicle make and year — no personal information, and nothing that identifies you or your device. Use of that service is governed by NHTSA's own policies. If you do not use the lookup, no network request is made.

Data storage and backups

Your data lives on your device. GarageKeeper lets you export your data (for example to a JSON, CSV, or printable report) and import it back. When you export or share a file, you choose where it goes through your device's standard share options — those exported files are under your control, not the developer's.

Data sharing

The developer does not receive, store, share, or sell any of your data. GarageKeeper contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data is stored only on your device, you are in full control of it. You can delete individual vehicles or records within the app, or remove all data by deleting the app. The developer holds no copy of your data and therefore cannot access or delete it on your behalf.

Children's privacy

GarageKeeper is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
